La Ristorante, Inc., based in Los Santos, San Andreas, and its subsidiaries franchise or operate a system of over 53,000 restaurants in 155 countries and territories under the company’s concepts – Cluckin' Bell, Well Stacked Pizza Co., and Burger shot. The Company’s La Ristorante, Cluckin' Bell brands are global leaders of the chicken, Mexican-style food, and pizza categories, respectively. Burger Shot is a fast-casual restaurant concept specializing in made-to-order chargrilled burgers, sandwiches and more.
